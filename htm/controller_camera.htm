Camera Switch

The Camera Switch button toggles the video source from the main camera to the external camera and back again.

Button Location and Label

       

Use

Press the Camera Switch button to switch from the main camera to the external camera. Press the Camera Switch button again to switch back from the external camera to the main camera.

Both the main and external cameras use the same video pair in the tether and only one camera can be enabled at a time. To use multiple cameras simultaneously, one camera must be configured to use the APIC (Auxiliary Pair of Independent Conductors) in the tether, and a separate interface must be used on the AUX port on the control panel.
