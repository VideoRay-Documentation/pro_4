VideoRay External Camera

The External Camera provides an additional viewpoint from the ROV. It can be directed 360 degrees horizontally, or aimed up or down.

Display

Main Camera Active

    

External Camera Active

Use

The VideoRay External Camera is activated by the Switch button in the upper right hand corner of the Camera instrument. When the external camera is active, the tilt and focus indictors and menu button are disabled.

The VideoRay External Camera is optional and may not be included in all VideoRay Pro 4 configurations.
