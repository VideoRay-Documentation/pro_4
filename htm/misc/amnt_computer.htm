Dell 4500 / 4600 Computer

The Dell 4600 has been set up with a "VideoRay" user account and there is no password assigned.

The mount that holds the computer to the control panel is designed to tilt to provide optimal viewing of the second monitor if one is available and to allow easier access to the side ports. To tilt the computer, lift it straight up and then pull it towards the operator. As it comes towards the operator, the back will start to drop. Continue pulling until the back rests on the control panel.

The Dell 4600 includes two USB 3.0 ports on the right hand side of the computer. These ports are not compatible with the VideoRay Pro 4 Ultra Lite control panel and should not be used to connect the computer to the control panel.

Computer Removal

  1. Remove the cables from the computer.
  2. Loosen the yellow knob at the center rear of the computer. The knob does not need to be removed completely.
  3. Push the computer towards the rear of the control panel.
  4. Lift the front of the computer from the panel and remove the computer with the tilt mount attached.
  5. To remove the tilt mount from the computer:
  • Remove the three screws that hold the front bracket to the tilt mount frame and remove the front bracket.
  • Lift the mount from the front of the computer to disengage the rear brackets from the computer and remove the mount.

    • Computer Installation

    1. Reverse the removal process listed above.
