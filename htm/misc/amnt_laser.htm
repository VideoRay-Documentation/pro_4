Laser Scaling Device

The laser scaling device can be used as a stand alone instrument, in which case it is always in the "On" state. It can also be used in conjunction with a PAM, which allows switching the beam On and Off remotely from the topide. For stand alone use, connect the cable directly to the ROV accessory port. For On/Off use, connect the cable to a generic PAM and connect the PAM to the ROV accessory port.

Direct connection of the laser to the ROV may facilitate diagnostics by separating it from the PAM and being able to isolate the troublesome component.
