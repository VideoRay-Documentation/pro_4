Manipulator

The manipulator head can be removed from the body by loosening the three socket head screws at the base of the head. Once the screws have been removed, the head can be pulled from the body.

The manipulator driver board and motor can be accessed by removing the knurled not at the rear of the manipulator. Once the nut is removed, the cable and end cap can be pulled from the body.

To remove the manipulator motor, unsolder the wires from the motor to the driver board. Unscrew the housing nut at the front of the body and remove the motor from the rear of the body.

Do not pull the end cap off too hard or too far out, or you may break the wires that connect to the driver board.
