Removal from the Mold

Repeat these steps for the male and female tether connectors.

  1. Once the Flexane has hardened, remove the connector from the mold.

  2. Cut the off the excess flashing using a straight edge and utility knife.

  3. Trim off any flashing left on the metal body of the connector.

  4. For PPT and Negative tether, cut the O-ring off the tether.
