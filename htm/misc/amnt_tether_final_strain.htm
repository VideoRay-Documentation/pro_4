Secure the Strain Reliefs in Position

Repeat these steps for the male and female tether connectors.

  1. Print the serial number and position the clear heat shrink about 2" behind molded connector (hidden under the strain relief).
  2. Place an oval threaded connector on the strain relief and position the strain relief to align the connector with the back of the molded connector.

  3. Tighten zip tie.

  4. Center the 3" section of 0.8" shrink tubing on the zip tie. Then wrap the tether strain relief with metal tape (three times around). This is to keep the strain relief from melting when the heat gun is used on the shrink tubing.

  5. Place metal tape on the other end of the shrink wrap, on the tether.

  6. Use a heat gun and shrink the heat shrink tubing. Be sure to not overheat (and possibly melt the strain relief mesh or tether jacket).

  7. Remove the metal tape.
