Tether Mold Preparation

  1. Coat both halves of the mold with a light coat of release agent and let them dry for 5 minutes.
  2. Insert the tether in the mold. For PPT and Negative Tether, the O-ring must be installed on the tether to seal the mold around the tether. For Neutral tether, the O-ring is not needed.

  3. Place the second half of the mold over the first and make sure the connection is centered in the mold.
  4. Tighten the mold retaining bolts.
