Solder the Connectors

Repeat these steps for the male and female tether connectors.

A pair of table vises works well to hold the tether and connector in place for soldering.

  1. Each wire from the connector needs to be soldered to the appropriate wire from the tether. Use the image and table below for reference.
  2. Slide a 1/2 inch (12 mm) piece of small diameter heat shrink over one wire from the connector and a short piece of large diameter heat shrink over the corresponding wire in the tether.

  3. Solder the wires one pair at a time.

  4. After soldering each wire pair, test the continuity before proceeding to the next wire pair.
  5. Slide the heat shrink pieces so that they overlap over the joint and apply heat to shrink the heat shrink wrap.
  6. Repeat the process for each wire.
  7. When all wires have been soldered, apply some rotation to the connector to slightly coil the wires and keep them compact.

  8. Test all connections for continuity and to ensure there are no shorts.
