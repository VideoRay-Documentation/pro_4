Strain Relief Preparation

Repeat these steps for each end of the tether to be terminated.

  1. Slide one piece of 0.8" x 3" shrink wrap tubing onto the tether.
  2. Attach binder clip to loop end of black mesh strain relief to prevent middle section of strain relief from sliding back through it.

  3. Push tether through middle opening of strain relief until the end of tether reaches the loop end.
  4. Spread mesh at the loop end and feed the tether through.
  5. Slide the strain relief down to the 0.8" shrink wrap tubing.
  6. Remove the binder clip.
  7. Install a zip tie 1" from bottom of black mesh strain relief through both layers of mesh and tie off, but do not tighten. Do not wrap the tie around the tether.

  8. Add oval threaded connector to mesh loop and close.
  9. Install the clear heat shrink. (this will cover the serial number)
  10. Slide the other length of shrink wrap onto the tether. (0.8" x 2" for Neutral tether, 0.4" x 2" for PPT and Negative tether)
