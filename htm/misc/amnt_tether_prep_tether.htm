Tether Preparation

Repeat these steps for each end of the tether to be terminated.

  1. Remove 6 inches (150 mm) of the tether jacket (and flotation material for PPT and Neutral tethers) being careful not to cut the wires or the Kevlar strand.

  2. Pull the Kevlar strands out of the way and restrain it with one hand.
  3. Cut the wires 1 inch (25mm) from the end of the jacket.

  4. Place O-ring over the tether and slide down over the Kevlar strands to hold them aside for later use.
  5. The O-ring will also need to be used later for the PPT and negative tethers.
  6. After stripping the wire ends, connect the two red wires to each other and the two black wires to each other and tin all wire ends with solder.
