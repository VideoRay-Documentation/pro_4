Introduction to Tactical Applications

Tactical Applications provide class participants with the opportunity to apply the knowledge and skills learned during the course to real-world situations. These scenarios challenge the participants to synthesize comprehensive methods from the individual class activities and complete a project from the planning phase through the final reporting phase.

In each case, the following should be addressed:

  • Selection and definition of the scenario

  • Planning
    • Objectives
    • Site assessment
    • Staff requirements
    • Equipment requirements
    • Logistics requirements

  • Field Execution
    • Deployment site selection
    • Staff scheduling
    • Data Collection
      • Still Images (above and below surface)
      • Video (above and below surface)
      • Logs

  • Project Reporting
    • Data Compilation
    • Record Keeping

  • Debriefing
    • What worked well or not
    • What should be changed and how
    • What remedial work is necessary
