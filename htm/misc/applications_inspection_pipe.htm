Tactical Applications - Pipe Inspection

Pipe penetration tactics are used to assess the conditions of pipes. Pipe inspections can be conducted on the exterior and/or interior of pipes. Examples might include inspections for structural or coating integrity, location surveys or determination of sediment levels.

Typical Challenges

  • Visibility
  • Current/Flow
  • Distance
  • Access points
  • Tight spaces
  • Portions of the pipe may be dry or clogged
