Editing Still Images

Generally, there are four areas where original images tend to suffer relative to the standards of high quality imagery: composition, size and quality. Being able to crop, scale and tune and annotate an image are considered fundamental skills of image editing.

In this exercise, participants will apply all three techniques to an image to improve its overall quality and add annotation to highlight a specific feature in the image.

There are many image editing tools available in the marketplace. An excellent and powerful image editing application that is available under the GNU General Public License (free) is Gimp. Information about Gimp can be found at: www.gimp.org
