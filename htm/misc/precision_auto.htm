Auto Pilot Modes

The VideoRay Pro 4 Ultra includes two auto pilot modes that can help pilots maintain position and/or proximity.

Auto Depth Exercise

  1. Using the mouse, click on the Auto Depth flag of the depth instrument to engage Auto Depth.
  2. Dive to a new depth.
  3. Center the depth control knob to allow auto depth to reengage.
  4. Allow Auto Depth to stabilize the depth of the ROV.
  5. Dive to a new depth.
  6. Center the depth control knob to allow auto depth to reengage.
  1. Using the mouse, drag the Auto Depth flag to a new depth.
  2. Allow Auto Depth to stabilize the depth of the ROV.
  3. Drag the Auto Depth flag to a new depth.

Auto Heading Exercise

  1. Using the mouse, click on the Auto Heading ring of the compass instrument to engage Auto Heading.
  2. Turn to a new heading.
  3. Center the joystick to allow auto heading to reengage.
  4. Allow Auto Heading to stabilize the heading of the ROV.
  5. Dive to a new depth.
  6. Center the depth control knob to allow auto depth to reengage.
  1. Using the mouse, drag the Auto Heading ring to a new heading.
    • Note that because the compass uses a heading up display, when you start to rotate the Auto Heading ring, the heading will change and the compass will start to rotate. Essentially, to achieve a specific heading, you will be trying to hit a moving target. It may be easier to turn to the desired heading using small increments of change.
  2. Allow Auto Heading to stabilize the heading of the ROV.
  3. Using the mouse, drag the Auto Heading ring to a new heading.
