YSI 600 XL Sonde

YSI 600XL

The YSI 600XL and YSI 600XLM compact sondes measure eleven parameters simultaneously:

  • Dissolved Oxygen

  • Temperature

  • Conductivity

  • Salinity

  • Specific Conductance

  • Resistivity

  • Depth or Shallow Vented Level

  • pH

  • ORP

  • Total Desolved Solids
